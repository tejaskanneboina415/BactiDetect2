How to Use This Tool

Follow these simple steps to classify your bacterial colony images:

  • Navigate to the Home page.
  • Click on the "Choose File" or the designated upload area.
  • Select a clear, well-lit image of a bacterial colony from your device.
  • Accepted image formats are: JPG, JPEG, PNG.
  • Once the file is selected, click the "Classify Colony" button.
  • The AI will process the image and display the predicted class along with a confidence score.

For best results, ensure your images are in focus and the colony features are distinct.
